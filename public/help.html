CalBridge Help

CalBridge helps you schedule meetings with calendar invites directly from Pumble.

Features:

  • Create meetings with calendar invites
  • Send invites via email (.ics format)
  • Sync with CalDAV calendars (optional)
  • Post notifications to Pumble channels

How to Use:

  1. Fill in the meeting details in the form
  2. Add attendee email addresses (one per line)
  3. Click "Create Meeting"
  4. Calendar invites will be sent to all attendees
